About Us

This is a sneaker and fashion product auction website where enthusiasts and collectors can discover a vast array of footwear and apparel. From the latest releases to rare, vintage finds, our platform offers products from top brands like Nike, Adidas, Jordan, and many more. Whether you are looking for the newest trends or timeless classics, our auctions provide an exciting and dynamic shopping experience. Our commitment is to connect you with the products you love in a fun and engaging way.

Mission

Bringing Vietnamese consumers Vietnamese products with international brand value.

Bringing comfort, style, and confidence to every step of our customers.

Vision

We dream of becoming a symbol of style and confidence in the world of fashion footwear. Our vision goes beyond merely creating quality shoes; we aim to foster a community that loves and creates around our brand.

Our Team

Đặng Tiến Minh - CEO

The CEO is responsible for the overall success of the company, playing a major role in defining the vision and mission. This role involves making critical decisions regarding the company's development direction, ensuring that the organization stays on track to achieve its long-term goals.

Nguyễn Hương Thơm - Strategic Management

The Strategic Management team focuses on formulating and implementing long-term strategies to achieve company goals. They conduct market research to identify opportunities and threats, and closely monitor performance to ensure continuous improvement. This team plays a crucial role in steering the company towards sustained growth and success.

Nguyễn Xuân Thu - COO-Chief Operating Officer

The COO oversees daily operations, ensuring that all processes are efficient and productive. They implement systems to enhance operational efficiency and manage budgets to ensure cost-effectiveness. The COO ensures that the company's day-to-day activities align with its strategic goals.

Nguyễn Phúc Hải Đăng - Product Manager

The Product Manager oversees the development of new products from concept to market launch. They conduct market research to understand customer needs, develop product strategies and roadmaps, and coordinate with various departments to ensure successful product launches. Additionally, the Product Manager analyzes product performance to gather feedback and drive continuous improvement.
